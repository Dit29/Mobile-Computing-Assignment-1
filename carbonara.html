Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Garofalo Spaghetti
  • Pure Olive Oil
  • Short Cut Bacon
  • Crushed Garlic Cloves
  • Chopped flat-leaf Parsley
  • Thickened Cream
  • Eggs
  • Egg Yolk
  • Parmesan
  • Mixed Salad Leaves

Directions

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large saucepan of boiling salted water until al dente. Drain.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a deep frying pan over high heat. Cook bacon for 4-5 mins or until golden and crisp.
  3. Add garlic and cook, stirring for 1 min. Add parsley and remove from heat.
  4. Whisk cream, eggs and yolk together in a jug. Add half of the parmesan and season with pepper.
  5. Return pasta to pan with cream mixture and toss to combine.
  6. Divide the spaghetti mixture among bowls. Top with remaining parmesan. Season with pepper and serve with salad.
